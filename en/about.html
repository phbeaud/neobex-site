What Defines Us

Our Mission

To provide Canadians with reliable, high-quality personal protective equipment at the best price. We help our clients save while ensuring their safety.

Our Vision

To become an indispensable reference in Canada for personal protection with innovative solutions adapted to professional needs.

At Neobex, we firmly believe that personal protection should never be a luxury. We focus on superior quality at the best price, with integrity and by prioritizing our clients' needs.

— Neobex Leadership
