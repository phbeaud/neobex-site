What Defines Us

We don't sell products. We build a better procurement system.

Our Mission

Restructure our clients' procurement so they pay the right price, use the right products, and simplify their purchasing management.

  • Detailed analysis of your supplies and invoices
  • Validated and compliant product equivalents
  • Cost, volume and logistics optimization
  • Flexible service, tailored per client and per site

Our Vision

To become the go-to partner for procurement optimization in Canada. Not the biggest catalog. Not the lowest price at any cost. The smartest approach.

Every business deserves a supply chain that works for them — not against them. Our role is to build that system behind the scenes, so our clients can focus on what matters: their operations.

We don't add a supplier to your list. We simplify that list.

— Neobex Team
